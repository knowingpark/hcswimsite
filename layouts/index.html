Welcome to the HC Swim Club

The Hutchins Collegiate Swim Club is a not-for-profit, family oriented, community club, open to all swimmers.
It was established by both The Hutchins School and St Michaels Collegiate School in 1990.
We are a vibrant swimming club that inspires, supports and prepares members to achieve their personal best, while striving for excellence within the sport of swimming.
Most of the information you need to know about the club can be found on this site. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us.
